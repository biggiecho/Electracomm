Frequently asked questions

The original FAQ had outdated timing. This version uses evergreen wording so it won’t look expired as quickly.

How does this preorder work?

Customers choose a model, storage size, finish, payment method, and delivery option. The site calculates their estimated total and reservation amount before submission.

Can I take a deposit instead of full payment?

Yes. This demo supports full payment, a 50% deposit, or a 25% reservation deposit. You can change those rules in the script to match your policy.

Where do order requests go right now?

In this frontend version, preorder requests are stored in the customer’s browser for demo purposes and a success message appears instantly. The next step is connecting the form to a backend or payment processor.

What should be added before launching publicly?

Add a real backend, payment checkout, confirmation emails or WhatsApp notifications, legal pages, and stock management so customers only see what is actually available.
